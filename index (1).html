Understanding Web Interactions

Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study! We're exploring how people interact with various website features and make decisions online. Your insights will help improve website designs and user experiences.

Before You Begin the Experiment

  1. Please read each step carefully—there are no right or wrong answers.
  2. You'll be using a ride‐booking site called Quick Taxi.

About Quick Taxi

On the Quick Taxi website, you can:

  • Book rides quickly and easily.
  • Find drivers near your location.
  • Let the website detect your location automatically for a faster booking experience, or manually type your location if you prefer.

Getting Started

  1. Visit the Website
    • After reading this page, click "Agree" and then "Next" to continue to the Quick Taxi website.
  2. Select Your Location
    • If you'd like the site to detect your location, you can use the "Use My Location" option. Otherwise, just type in your pick‐up and drop‐off addresses .
  3. Book Your Ride
    • Click "Book Now" to confirm your trip.

Complete the Survey

  • Once your booking is confirmed, you'll be directed to a short survey about your experience. After finishing the survey, you'll find instructions on how to return to Prolific and collect your reward.

Confidentiality

  • Your responses will remain anonymous and will only be used for research purposes.

  • All collected data will be kept confidential.

Consent

By continuing, you confirm that you meet the minimum age requirements for data processing consent in your country. You also grant permission for the University of California, Berkeley to use your responses in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

If you wish to withdraw your consent or request the deletion of your responses, please contact us at egelman@icsi.berkeley.edu.
